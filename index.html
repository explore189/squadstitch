About us

Built for teams that need easy, reliable jersey ordering.

SquadStitch helps teams create custom jerseys without the back-and-forth confusion. We support design, sizing, names, numbers, colours and team-ready order coordination. Whether you need a full cricket kit or a basketball jersey set, we make the process simple from concept to delivery.

Why Tirupur

Connected to a proven knitwear manufacturing hub.

Tirupur, Tamil Nadu is widely known as one of India’s major knitwear and garment manufacturing centres. For teams, that means access to experienced production networks for sportswear, teamwear and custom apparel.

Website wording is kept professional and inclusive for Canadian audiences: the focus is on manufacturing expertise, materials and reliability.
